Creative Development & Review Process

Noise Media · Internal Process Document
1
Step 1
Briefing & Alignment
  • Account Manager (AM) leads a clear creative brief session with the creative team
  • Brief must cover:
    • Objectives and KPIs
    • Deliverables and formats
    • Role of content within the wider strategy
    • Key messaging and guardrails
Output: Shared understanding of what success looks like
2
Step 2
Ideation & Direction Setting
  • Creative team runs a brainstorm session
    • Attendees: AM and/or relevant strategist, paid media lead, or account team member where makes sense
  • Ideas are:
    • Discussed, refined and challenged in the room
    • Narrowed down to a clear set of routes to progress
Output: Agreed creative directions to take forward
If needed
3
Step 3 · If needed
Cross-Team Validation
  • If no team members outside of creative were present (e.g. account, paid, strategy):
    • A short follow-up session is held to review and align on directions
  • Ensures:
    • Ideas match the brief
    • Channel considerations are accounted for
    • No misalignment later in the process
Output: Final confirmed routes for development
4
Step 4
Midway Checkpoint Setup
  • AM defines a midway review point
    • Typically ~50% of time to final delivery
  • Purpose:
    • Sense-check one developed direction
    • Avoid late-stage rework
5
Step 5
Creative Development (Work in Progress Review)
  • Creative team develops the work to a clear directional stage:
    • Not fully polished
    • But strong enough to show concept, structure and execution
  • Internal review includes:
    • Creative lead (e.g. Beau) alignment
    • Quick review with AM / strategy / paid
  • Review outcome:
    Proceed as planned
    Minor tweaks and continue
    Flag gaps or misalignment early
Output: Confidence in direction before final production
6
Step 6
Final Development & Delivery
  • Creative team completes assets following aligned direction
  • AM ensures:
    • Output meets brief
    • Ready for client delivery

Discrepancy & Escalation Process

A. Post-Brainstorm Misalignment

If AM and creative team disagree on direction:
1
Escalate to Account Director (e.g. Sam or Matt)
2
Attempt to resolve through discussion and refinement
3
If still unresolved → escalate to Executive Leadership (Ant or Joe) for final decision on whether to proceed with an idea

B. Misalignment During Development

If AM is not aligned on WIP creative:
1
Escalate to Account Director for review
2
Align on changes or direction
3
If still unresolved → escalate to Ant or Joe

C. New Ideas Introduced Mid-Process

If new creative routes or ideas emerge:
!
AM must be consulted before progressing
  • Ensures:
  • Alignment with brief
  • No scope or strategy drift
