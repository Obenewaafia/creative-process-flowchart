Internal Process

Creative Development
& Review Process

How creative and account teams work together — from brief to delivery, with clear checkpoints and escalation paths.

1
📋
Step 1
Briefing & Alignment
Lead
Account Manager leads a clear creative brief session with the creative team
Covers
Objectives & KPIs, deliverables & formats, role of content within the wider strategy, key messaging & guardrails
→ Shared understanding of what success looks like
2
💡
Step 2
Ideation & Direction Setting
Lead
Creative team runs a brainstorm session
Attendees
AM and/or relevant strategist, paid media lead, or account team member where it makes sense
Process
Ideas discussed, refined and challenged in the room. Narrowed down to a clear set of routes to progress.
→ Agreed creative directions to take forward
If no cross-team members were present
3
🤝
Step 3 · If Needed
Cross-Team Validation
When
If no team members outside creative (account, paid, strategy) were present at ideation
Action
A short follow-up session is held to review and align on directions
Ensures
Ideas match the brief, channel considerations are accounted for, no misalignment later in the process
→ Final confirmed routes for development
4
⏰
Step 4
Midway Checkpoint Setup
Lead
AM defines a midway review point — typically ~50% of time to final delivery
Purpose
Sense-check one developed direction. Avoid late-stage rework.
→ Checkpoint scheduled at the halfway mark
5
🔨
Step 5
Creative Development (WIP Review)
Stage
Creative team develops work to a clear directional stage — not fully polished, but strong enough to show concept, structure and execution
Review
Creative lead (e.g. Beau) alignment, then quick review with AM / strategy / paid
Outcomes
✅Proceed as planned
🔄Minor tweaks & continue
⚠️Flag gaps early
→ Confidence in direction before final production
6
🚀
Step 6
Final Development & Delivery
Creative
Creative team completes assets following the aligned direction
AM checks
Output meets brief and is ready for client delivery
→ Final assets delivered to client

Discrepancy & Escalation

What happens when things don't align — clear paths to resolution

A Post-Brainstorm Misalignment

If AM and creative team disagree on direction:

1
Escalate to Account Director (Sam or Matt)
2
Attempt to resolve through discussion and refinement
3
If still unresolved → escalate to Executive Leadership (Ant or Joe) for final decision

B Misalignment During Development

If AM is not aligned on WIP creative:

1
Escalate to Account Director for review
2
Align on changes or direction
3
If still unresolved → escalate to Ant or Joe

C New Ideas Introduced Mid-Process

If new creative routes or ideas emerge during development:

!
AM must be consulted before progressing
✓
Ensures alignment with brief and prevents scope or strategy drift
